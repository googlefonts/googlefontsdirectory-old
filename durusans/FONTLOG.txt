Duru Sans Font Information

Duru Sans is low contrast a classic 20th century style 
sans design. Duru Sans is a new take on mixing the 
humanist urge with the modernist one. Duru Sans also 
somehow manages to be elegant and a workhorse type at 
the same time. Duru Sans can be used at a wide range 
of sizes.

Duru Sans is a Unicode typeface family that supports 
languages that use the Latin script and its variants, and 
could be expanded to support other scripts.

More specifically, this release supports the following 
Unicode ranges: Latin-1
