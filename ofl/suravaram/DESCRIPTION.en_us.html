Suravaram is a brush script font, suitable for headings, posters, invitations and anywhere you want to use a handwriting style. It is named after Suravaram Gurajada, whose literature and poetry enriched the Telugu people.

Designed by Purushoth Kumar and developed by Ambarisha Darbha in 2013 and made available by Silicon Andhra under the SIL Open Font License v1.1. The Suravaram project is led by Ambarisha Darbha, a type designer based in Hyderabad, India. To contribute, see github.com/appajid/suravaram
