FONTLOG for the Average Sans fonts

This file provides detailed information on the Average Sans Font Software.

This information should be distributed along with the Average Sans fonts
and any derivative works.

Basic Font Information

Average Sans has the color, structure and proportions of its serif sister
family, Average, with which it complements harmoniously. The neutrality of the
forms in the serif creates a nice texture, both for texts and short headlines.
Try it on texts for for online magazines, academic texts and blogs.

To contribute to the project contact Eduardo Tunni at edu@tipo.net.ar
