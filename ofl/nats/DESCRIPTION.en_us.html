NATS is a Telugu font.

The Telugu is designed by Purushoth Kumar and developed by Ambarisha Darbha in 2012 and made available under the SIL Open Font License v1.1 by Silicon Andhra. The Latin is designed by Julieta Ulanovsky and originally published as Montserrat. The NATS project is led by Ambarisha Darbha, a type designer based in Hyderabad, India. To contribute, see github.com/appajid/nats
