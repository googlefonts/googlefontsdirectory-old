TharLon (Myanmar)

TharLon is an authentic Myanmar typeface which covers several languages, such as Burmese, Mon, Shan, Karen and Karenni, for use on the web and all other digital typography software. TharLon is the name of the King who standardized the unit of measurement in law during the ancient Burmese Kingdom Era.

The TharLon font is designed by Ngwe Tun, with Reserved Font Name 'TharLon'. It builds on Sai Zin Di Di Zone's Yunghkio, and Arimo.

Read more about the TharLon font project at www.tharlon.org.
