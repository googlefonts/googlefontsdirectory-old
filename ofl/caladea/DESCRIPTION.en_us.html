Caladea is a modern Latin typeface inspired by the contrast, style and ornaments of Khmer typefaces and writing styles. Its main objective is to be used to write Latin texts in a Khmer context, but it is also an elegant choice for all kinds of texts.

Caladea is a reworking of Cambo into a family.

Designed by Carolina Giovagnoli and Andrés Torresi for Huerta Tipográfica.
