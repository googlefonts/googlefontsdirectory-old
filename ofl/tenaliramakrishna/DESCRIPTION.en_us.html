Tenali Ramakrishna is a Telugu font.

Designed by Ambarisha Darbha in 2013 and made available by Silicon Andhra under the SIL Open Font License v1.1. The Tenali Ramakrishna project is led by Ambarisha Darbha, a type designer based in Hyderabad, India. To contribute, see github.com/appajid/tenaliramakrishna
