Tenali Ramakrishna is a Telugu font.

The Telugu is designed by Ambarisha Darbha in 2013 and made available by Silicon Andhra under the SIL Open Font License v1.1. The Latin is designed by Wojciech Kalinowski and originally published as Classica. The Tenali Ramakrishna project is led by Ambarisha Darbha, a type designer based in Hyderabad, India. To contribute, see github.com/appajid/tenaliramakrishna
