Palanquin is a Unicode-compliant Latin and Devanagari text type family designed for the digital age. The Devanagari is monolinear and was designed alongside the sans serif Latin. It currently comprises of five text weights, and will be extended to ten. Palanquin is versatile and strikes a balance between typographic conventions and that bit of sparkle.
