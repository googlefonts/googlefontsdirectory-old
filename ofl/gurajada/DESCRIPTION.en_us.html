Gurajada is Telugu handwriting font, suitable for headings, posters, and invitations.

The Telugu is designed by Purushoth Kumar and developed by Appaji Ambarisha Darbha in 2013 and made available by Silicon Andhra under the SIL Open Font License v1.1. The Latin is developed by Juan Pablo del Peral and originally published as Alegreya Sans. The Gurajada project is led by Appaji Ambarisha Darbha, a type designer based in Hyderabad, India. To contribute, see github.com/appajid/gurajada
