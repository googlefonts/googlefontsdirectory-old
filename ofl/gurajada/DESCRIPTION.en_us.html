Gurajada is Telugu handwriting font.

Designed by Purushoth Kumar and developed by Appaji Ambarisha Darbha in 2013 and made available by Silicon Andhra under the SIL Open Font License v1.1. The Gurajada project is led by Appaji Ambarisha Darbha, a type designer based in Hyderabad, India. To contribute, see github.com/appajid/gurajada
