and any derivative works.

Basic Font Information

Unna is the surname of the mother of its type designer, 
Jorge de Buen. Her soft look is expressed through 
delicated serifs and strong stems, that accentuating 
the typical neoclassical vertical texture.

Unna is a Unicode typeface family that supports languages that
use the Latin script and its variants, and could be expanded 
to support other scripts.

More specifically, this release supports the following 
Unicode ranges: Latin-1
