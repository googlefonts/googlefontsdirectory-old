Encode Sans is a versatile workhorse sans serif design, featuring a huge range of weights and widths. It's ready for all kind of typographic challenges.

This is the wide family, which is part of the superfamily with Compressed, Condensed, Narrow, Normal and Wide families, each with 9 weights.

This project is led by Impallari Type, a foundry based in Rosario, Argentina. To contribute, see Encode on the Impallari Projects site.
