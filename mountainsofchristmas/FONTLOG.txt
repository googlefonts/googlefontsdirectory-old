Basic Font Information

Ever wonder what font sugarplum fairies dream about? Mountains of Christmas by the talented lettering artist Crystal Kluge of the Tart Workshop font foundry! Mountains of Christmas is the perfect casual style playful serif font when you want to give your words a warm personal touch, and it's just plain fun! Enjoy Mountains of Christmas throughout the year and visit the Tart Workshop year round for more amazing fun and flirty handmade fonts and custom lettering!

Designed by Crystal Kluge of Tart Workshop (a DBA of Font Diner, Inc)

Mountains of Christmas Bold is a Unicode typeface family that supports 
languages that use the Latin script and its variants, and 
could be expanded to support other scripts.

More specifically, this release supports the following Unicode
ranges: Latin-1

To contribute to the project contact the Font Diner at 
support@fontdiner.com
